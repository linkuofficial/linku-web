About LINKU

An early-stage technology venture at the boundary of artificial intelligence and the physical world.

LINKU is an early-stage technology venture working at the boundary of artificial intelligence and the physical world. We build systems that don't just compute, but perceive, decide, and act in reality.

Our focus is edge AI, control systems, and small, customizable intelligent machines. Our method is software–hardware co-design: we pair low-cost, off-the-shelf hardware with compact, scene-optimized edge models, so the complete system outperforms conventional setups running on comparable hardware. The aim is practical, deployable intelligence for specialized real-world settings — not bigger models, but smarter whole systems.

Our first public project is Neblux, a live, interactive knowledge graph at the edge of intelligence and infrastructure. More is on the way.
