Technology

Intelligence, made to fit.

LINKU integrates AI and control into compact machines — built for your setting, running on-site, and improving over time.

Built for you

Every system is built for a single task. From sensing to model, it is tuned to your setting — fitting real needs and constraints, and adapting as they evolve.

Runs on-site

Computation and decisions happen on the device itself, with no cloud round-trip and no wait on a connection. Response is immediate, operation stays reliable offline, and your data remains on-site — where speed and security matter most.

Value by design

Performance is not dictated by hardware specifications alone. Through software–hardware co-design, software unlocks the hardware's full potential, turning a sensible investment into performance beyond its spec. What we deliver is the outcome that matters.

Better with use

Once deployed, the system keeps learning from real-world settings — its judgment sharpening and its fit to your environment deepening over time. The longer it runs, the more it is worth.
